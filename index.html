Introduction
Welcome! This experiment contains 20 search tasks, each of which will take you 1-2 minutes.
In each task, you will be given a topic to search for. The system will suggest different terms related to that topic based on its keywords. Feel free to select as many terms as you like and search until you are happy with the search results.
Please follow the instructions below to start the experiment with a sample task:
Step 1 - Select the terms you think are relevant to the topic by clicking on them.
Step 2 - Click the search button to review the search results.
Step 3 - If the search results are unsatisfactory, you can adjust the selected terms as many times as you like.
Step 4 - Once you think you have finished this search task, click the Next button at the bottom right of the page.
Tips:
*Please do not type in the search bar. All you need to do is select the term below.
*The terms you have selected will be displayed in the search bar. To deselect them, simply click on the terms again.
*Scroll down the screen to ensure that you can view all the terms in each task.
*If you have any problems at any time during the experiment, please feel free to contact the experimenter.
